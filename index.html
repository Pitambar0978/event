About the Hackathon

The Sai Nath University Hackathon is a premier innovation event that brings together the brightest minds from across India to collaborate, code, and create groundbreaking solutions. Over three action-packed days, participants will work in teams to tackle real-world challenges in fields like AI, IoT, Web Development, Blockchain, Gaming, Video Editing and many more.

Theme & Focus Areas

Main Theme: Innovating for a Better Tomorrow

  • Smart Education & E-Learning
  • Sustainable Agriculture & Rural Development
  • FinTech & Digital Inclusion
  • Healthcare & Wellness Tech
  • AI, Machine Learning, and Data Science Solutions
  • Open Innovation (Participant’s Choice)

Why Participate?

  • 💡 Learn & Build – Gain hands-on experience with modern tools.
  • 🏆 Compete & Win – Exciting prizes, cash rewards, and goodies.
  • 🌐 Network & Collaborate – Connect with mentors & experts.
  • 📜 Certificates & Recognition – For all participants.
  • 🚀 Startup Opportunities – Pitch your idea to investors.

Event Details

Dates: 11th–13th September 2025

Venue: Sai Nath University, Ranchi

Eligibility: Students from any University of Ranchi

Team Size: 4–5 members

Event Schedule

  • Day 1 (11 Sept) – Inauguration,Hackethon , Video Editing .
  • Day 2 (12 Sept) – Gaming,Cosmetic Play.
  • Day 3 (13 Sept) – Inter- college Dance and Singing, prize distribution.
